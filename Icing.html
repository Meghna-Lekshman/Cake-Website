Strawberry Buttercream Icing Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Powdered Sugar 4 cups
Butter 1 cup
Pureed strawberries 1/4 cup
Decorating Bag 1
Decorating Tip 1
Mixer 1

Instructions

  1. Mix Powdered Sugar and Butter in a mixer on low.
  2. Add Strawberry Puree and mix until the right consistency.
